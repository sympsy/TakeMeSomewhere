Iconic Experiences

The Sunset in Oia

This is a must-do. The sunsets here are world-renowned. Find a good spot, whether it's at the Oia Castle ruins or a less crowded viewpoint.

Catamaran Cruise

Sail around the caldera, swim in the crystal-clear waters, and enjoy views of the volcanic islands, Red Beach, and White Beach. Many cruises include meals and drinks.

Caldera Trail

Walk from Fira to Oia for breathtaking panoramic views of the caldera. It's a moderately challenging hike, but the scenery is worth it.

Wine Tasting

Santorini is known for its unique volcanic wines. Take a wine tour to visit local wineries and sample their distinctive flavors.
